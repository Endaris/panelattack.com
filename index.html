A Modern Classic

Panel Attack is a free puzzle game inspired by popular games such as Tetris Attack and Pokemon Puzzle League while still maintaining authentic mechanics. Arrange colored panels in rows and columns of three or more to match matches that clear. Panels then fall from gravity and can make chains that give bonuses or attack the other player.

Custom Mods

You can also customize graphics, and sound to add in your favorite characters or go for that retro feel from the classic games.

Low Latency Seamless Online Multiplayer

Panel Attack was designed from the ground up to have low latency input and fast online networking with rollback net code to make playing across the globe seamless.
