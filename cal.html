Panel Attack/ Panel de Pon Tournaments

Our tournaments are mostly hosted on our Challonge page. As a general rule of thumb, we host Panel de Pon tournaments using Nintendo Switch Online every first and third weekend of the month, and Panel Attack tournaments every second and fourth weekends. Please refer to the calendar below for the most up to date details, and the associated discord page for additional information or questions.
